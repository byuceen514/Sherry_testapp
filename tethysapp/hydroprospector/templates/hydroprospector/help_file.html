HydroProspector Help File

Purpose of the Application

The purpose of the HydroProspector app is calculate the reservior area and respective volume of water associated with a user-designed dam site and dam height within Yichang, China and the surrounding region. 

User Interface

Figure 1: User Interface

How to Use the App

The first step is to select a desired dam loacation on the map. User can zoom to a river by clicking on the map, or by clicking the plus and minus buttons in the upper left corner of the map. Once zoomed in, click exactly on the river at the desired location for a dam.  After clicking, a point will display in the location where the mouse was clicked indicating the potential dam location, shown as Figure 2.

Figure 2: Click on the river to select dam location

The next step is is to type in the dam height in the bar provided in the upper left corner of the page, shown as Figure 3.

Figure 3: Input dam height and click on run botton

Once the river has been selected, click the "Run HydroProspector" button to calculate. The calculation status will shown under the button. Once the status become "Succeed!", the reservoir and the watershed associated with the potential dam will be displayed on the map, while the volume of the reservoir will be displayed under the button, shown as Figure 4:

Figure 4: Results

Use/Restrictions

The app currently is only functional in the region covered by the DEM.  The app will not function properly if a river outside of the DEM is selected.
